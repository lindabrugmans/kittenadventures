Tutormeow

1. Find something to climb on, lets take a high fence.
2. Get ready.. lean onto your front legs anddd take off!! (no ur not a plane. Meow)
3. Jump onto the fence, and push yourself upwards with your hind legs.
4. When your hind legs reach your front legs, you want to push yourself upwards AGAIN with your hind legs.
5. Make yourself as tall ass posible, and grab the end of the fence with your front legs.
6. Place your hind legs against the fence, and pull yourself up onto the fence. You are now a meowster at climbing high fences!
